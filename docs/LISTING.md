## EN — English (default)

### Name
PersonalHygiene

### Subtitle (30)
Daily routines, kept on-device

### Promotional Text (170)
Plan your day with military precision. Medication reminders that respect Silent mode (Critical Alerts). All your data stays on your iPhone + iCloud.

### Description (~1 800)
PersonalHygiene is a focused daily-routine app for one person who wants reliable structure without surrendering their data.

YOUR DAY, BLOCKED OUT
• Drag time blocks to plan medication, sleep, focus, hydration, and any other recurring habit.
• Notifications fire 15 minutes before every block. Snooze, skip-once, mark-done — right from the lock screen.
• Apple Watch complication shows your next block + day-completion %.

MEDICATIONS YOU WON'T MISS
• Critical Alerts cut through Silent + Focus modes for medication blocks (with your explicit permission).
• Adherence chart, dose history, 30-day CSV export.
• Skip-dose, snooze, missed-dose probe.

SLEEP + MOOD + HYDRATION
• Auto-bedtime calculator from your day's plan.
• Sleep debt tracker with weekly trend.
• 30-day mood heatmap with weekly delta.
• Hydration goal with daily streaks.

VACATIONS, END-TO-END
• Trips with escalating reminders (6 months → day-of).
• AI itinerary wizard: 5-question prompt that runs on Apple Intelligence on-device, or copies to Claude.ai / ChatGPT / Perplexity.
• Marine forecast for diving days. Currency conversion. Travel advisories from 5 government sources.
• Document scanner stores passports in your Keychain — offline-accessible during the trip.

PRIVACY YOU CAN VERIFY
• No analytics. No third-party SDKs. No advertising identifiers.
• Your data lives in your iPhone + your iCloud private database. The developer cannot read or recover it.
• Backup as a JSON file you control. Restore on any device.

LANGUAGES
• English. Spanish. French. All UI strings, all data.

### Keywords (100)
routine,medication,reminder,vacation,itinerary,focus,sleep,hydration,birthday,critical,alert,private

### What's New (v1.0)
First public release.
• Daily routine with templates for weekdays + weekends.
• Medication blocks with Critical Alerts + adherence chart.
• Sleep auto-bedtime + sleep debt.
• Apple Watch app + complication.
• Hydration, mood, housekeeping, birthdays modules.
• Vacation module with AI itinerary wizard, document scanner, currency, marine forecast, advisories.
• Full ES + EN + FR localization.
• Privacy: zero telemetry, no third-party SDKs, all data in your Apple ecosystem.
